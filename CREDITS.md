## Color Palette

Palette built using [Aether](https://github.com/bjarneo/aether), a palette-first
theme generator. Terminal colors are defined in `colors.toml` and consumed by
Omarchy's template system.

## Tooling

- [Omarchy](https://omarchy.org/) — Arch-based Hyprland distribution and theme system
- [Aether](https://github.com/bjarneo/aether) — palette/theme generator
